Library

BPIT library is fully air-conditioned with the state of art facilities, spacious reading rooms, separate computer rooms for students and faculty and a rich collection of books and periodicals including e-journals. It is fully automated and Wi-Fi enabled with online reprography facility.

BPIT being a member of J-Gate, Emarald and IEEE has access to full ASSP package of 145 e-journals. The library remains open beyond institute timings.
